## Step-by-Step Usage

### Adding FFA Spawns
1. Switch to "FFA Positions" mode (shift-click wand)
2. Walk/fly to desired spawn location
3. **Right-click on a block**
4. New spawn marker appears with player head and red boots

### Removing Specific Spawn ‚≠ê BEST METHOD
1. Look at the spawn marker you want to remove (from any distance)
2. **Right-click directly on the armor stand**
3. Spawn is removed instantly
4. Perfect for removing specific spawns while flying

### Removing Last Spawn
1. Simply **left-click** anywhere
2. Most recent spawn is removed

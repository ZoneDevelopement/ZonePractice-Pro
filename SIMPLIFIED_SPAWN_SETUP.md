### For Standard Arena Setup (Duel/1v1)

When you're in **Positions** mode:

#### Setting Spawn Positions
- **Left-click on a BLOCK** ‚Üí Sets Position 1 (Blue spawn)
- **Right-click on a BLOCK** ‚Üí Sets Position 2 (Red spawn)
- Markers appear to show where spawns are
- Markers are visual indicators only (cannot be clicked to remove)

### For FFA Arena Setup

When you're in **FFA Positions** mode:

#### Adding Spawns
- **Right-click on a BLOCK** ‚Üí Adds new spawn at that location
- Marker appears immediately with label "FFA Spawn #X (Right-click to remove)"

#### Removing Specific Spawn
- **Right-click on an ARMOR STAND** ‚Üí Removes that spawn
- Works from **ANY distance** (even while flying!)
- Just look at the marker and click it

#### Removing Last Spawn
- **Left-click anywhere** ‚Üí Removes the last spawn you added
